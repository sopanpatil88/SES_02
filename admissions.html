ADMISSIONS

Join the Shantiniketan Family

We welcome students who are eager to learn, grow, and contribute to our vibrant community.

Admission Process

Step 1

Inquiry

Submit an inquiry form or visit our campus to learn more about our programs.

Step 2

Campus Visit

Schedule a guided tour to experience our facilities and meet our faculty.

Step 3

Application

Complete the application form with required documents and photographs.

Step 4

Assessment

Age‑appropriate interaction and assessment to understand your child's needs.

Step 5

Enrollment

Complete admission formalities and welcome your child to Shantiniketan.

Age Criteria

Jr. KG 3+ years
Sr. KG 4+ years
1st Std 5+ years
2nd – 5th As per age
6th – 10th As per age + Assessment

* Age calculated as of June 1st of the academic year.
